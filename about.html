The club

Where the fastest sport in the world
meets the slowest island.

A clubhouse, not a court rental.

Padel Guru was built on a simple read of what was missing in Bali. There were courts, scattered, often outdoor, often hard to find, often rented out by the hour with nothing else around. There was no club.

We changed that. Five courts under one cathedral roof. A café that opens at six and stays open after the floodlights are off. A sauna for the morning regulars and a recliner lounge for the families that come to watch. A proshop with rackets you can actually demo before you commit.

The point was never just padel. The point was a place where padel happens, and where the rest of the day still feels good.

What we do differently

Three things matter.
The rest is detail.

01

Tournament-spec courts.
Always.

Real grass, real glass, regulation dimensions, regulation lighting. The same surface you'd find at WPT events. Maintained by hand every morning before the doors open.

02

Booking in under a minute.

Pick a date, pick a time, you're booked. No phone calls, no WhatsApp threads with the club manager, no waiting for confirmation. Show up and play.

03

A real clubhouse.

Café from six, bar from sunset, sauna and showers any time. The day after the match should be as good as the match itself. We built the building around that idea.

For everyone

Beginners welcome.
Pros, even more so.

Free 30-minute intro sessions for first-timers, every Saturday morning. Drop in. Borrow a racket. Try the fastest-growing sport in the world, on what we think are the best courts in Indonesia.

For regulars: open mixers every Wednesday and Friday evening. Show up alone, leave with four new friends and a cold drink.
